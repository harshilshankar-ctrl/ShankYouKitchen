Tips:
- Square-ish crops look best in the collage and polaroids.
- Daylight near a window, no flash. Vibrant > accurate.
- NO fried food, no fries — nothing that wouldn't survive a fridge.
- NO wine glasses anywhere. Ever.
- No people in shots. The food and the kitchen are the subjects.
- Stock placeholders load until your files exist (needs internet).
